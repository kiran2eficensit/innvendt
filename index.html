CEGMA Earth E5

This flagship offering from Innvendt Insights is an Internet connected Earth Leakage Monitoring sensor

Monitor faults through a comprehensive visual dashboard with data coming in from machinery connected to the CEGMA Earth sensors.

Alerts would be triggered through email, SMS and a call in case an immediate action is required.

IP rated plug and play sensors are connected to various electrical end points. These sensors are modular, yet robust, with the ability to interface to the internet through WiFi, Ethernet or Cellular.

Powerful data driven diagnostic tools enable you to view and analyses data originating from multiple devices and identify anomalies. This helps you to pro actively protect expensive machinery from damages.

Role based access control with several other Enterprise ready features enable configuration and management of sensors and users at scale, spread across multiple geographies and departments.
